Industrial Training Modules

ITM course serves as a hot melting pot of the dynamically changing needs of the industry. These modules are designed in collaboration with top industry and academic experts across various fields. A short term ITM is typically held for 2-3 days, whereas a full course in ITM requires 5-7 days depending upon the nature of training required.

Circuit Branches:

  • Artificial Intelligence
  • Machine learning
  • Semiconductor Technology (material selection, fabrication, designing and packaging
  • Semiconductors in emerging technical drive through
  • Data mining and analytics (Scope and Impact)

Non-Circuit Branches:

  • Automobile Design
  • Strength of Materials
  • Mechanical failures and their analysis

Inter-Disciplinary:

  • Nano science an emerging field
  • Simulation in various branches of engineering and science
  • Theoretical study approach in various fields related to science and engineering

The entire training module is structured to reflect an extra ordinary show of theoretical understanding, live and practical demonstration of the concepts and honing the industrially relevant skillsets of the participants.

Plug n Play Workshop in Schools

There are various interesting topics in science and mathematics which need an “Out of the Book” treatment. Under a typical classroom session with a prescribed format of syllabus and set examination pattern, it becomes nearly impossible on the educator’s end to bring the true joy of learning these concepts. TOSE acknowledges the limitations of system and is dedicated to workout many exciting and enjoyable learning package solution at school level.

Mathematics:

  • Understanding number theory and system
  • Basic geometry and related theorems
  • Nature of algebraic equation and their Handling

Physics:

  • Mechanics elaborate session (theory+numericals+practical models etc.)
  • Understanding nature of Gravity (freefall, blackhole)
  • Heat and Temperature
  • Mathematical methods in Physics

Chemistry:

  • World of Chemical reactions
  • World of organic chemistry

Biology:

  • World of Cell biology
  • Evolution theory or a fact

Miscellaneous:

  • Technology impacting human lives

TOSE-TALK

TOSE-Talk is an opportunity to listen to the first-hand account of the scientific discoveries. Scientists, engineers and professors working in reputed research labs and academics will be invited to deliver a general public talk to a wide range of audience.

Nature of this talk will be introductory and meant primarily to incite a threshold curiosity among the audience about their research work. The content delivered will be well within the limits of a 101 course, which means no prior exposure to the subject is expected from the audience.

Tentative topics:


  • Science of climate change
  • Life on Mars, a feasibility report
  • Neuroscience the unexplored world
  • Evolution, Homo-sapiens to Robo-sapiens
  • Human genome
  • Space-time
  • Universe micro to macro
  • Theory of everything
  • Quantum computing
  • Time travel
  • Science of birth and death
  • Origin of universe
  • Origin of life
  • Origin of civilization
  • Origin of language, art and exhibitions

Career Mantra@TOSE

Whether you are a school, college or a university level student, Career Mantra@TOSE gives you a personalised interactive session in any area of science and engineering which you would like to get to the bottom of. All you need to do is to signup for the Career Mantra@TOSE by filling up the necessary questionnaire and we will get back to you with the best possible career expert in the related field.

Why attending a Career Mantra@TOSE is a must for all those aspiring to build a career in any field of science and engineering?


  • You get a complete roadmap to build your career in the desired field
  • You get a reality check between your aspirational and the professional journey when you speak to the field expert.
  • You are empowered to take important career decisions on your own wisdom without being trapped into hoax and hypes.
