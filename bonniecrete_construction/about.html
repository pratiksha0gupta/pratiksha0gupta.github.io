About Our Company

Your Sunken Concrete Specialists

We specialize in Lifting sunken concrete. Whether it's a Driveway, Sidewalk, Garage Floor, Stairs, Porch/Addition, Warehouse or Office Floor. Interior/Exterior or a Pad with a Building on it? Size and Thickness Don't Matter. We Lift them All.

Experienced

Over 30 years of Concrete experience, operating in the lower mainland for the last 15 years Bonniecrete Construction Ltd. has become a trusted name in Concrete Restoration. We take pride in our work and back it up with a 5 year Warranty.  

We care about the Environment

Our lifting process is Environmentally Friendly. Lifting is accomplished by drilling holes in the concrete pad, approximately every 4 feet then Hydraulically pumping a mix of Sand, Cement, Water and Bentonite clay. Once the slab is back in place we fill the holes with grout or grout and rocks in the case of exposed aggregate slabs. The water then evaporates and the cement hardens leaving solid ground beneath your slab where once it was hollow. This also has the effect of stabalizing the slab.

Small Concrete Removal and Replacement

Though we specialize in raising your sunken slabs we also take on small concrete removal and placement jobs. Whether your concrete is just too worn and broken to be lifted or you want a new sidewalk? Even though we do this type of work less frequently than Lifting, it still receives the same attention to detail as all our other endeavours.
